Welcome to four3four. Here at your fingertips is everything you'll need during your stay with us — from finding the Wifi password straight through to checking out, this guide has it all.

Essentials

Lockbox

We have included the lockbox code in the check-in instructions guide on Airbnb, but in case you can't find it or can't remember it, the code is 4159. If you are having any issues, please contact us or the security office for assistance.

Wifi

The Wifi router is located in the lounge, on the console. Should you need to restart the router or if the green indicator light is not showing, press the power button to turn off the unit, wait a minute and then press the same button to turn it back on again. Should you not come right, get in touch with us.

  • Network name: A434
  • Password: UnitAfour34wifi

Netflix

Netflix is avaliable in the apartment, we've already signed you in - just use the 434 guests account to start binge watching.

Safe

We have installed a safe for your convenience inside the cupboard of the main bedroom - to reset the access code and to use the safe, follow the instructions listed on the safe.

Self-service launderette

There is a self-service coin-operated laundry, with washing machines and dryers, behind the lifts in the upper garage of B Block which is open to residents at all hours - to access this service, take the elevator in the B Block down to level G.

Quiet hours

No noise should be made, that is audible outside of the apartment, during the following “quiet times”, as set by the building's guidelines — 20:00 to 08:00 from Sunday to Thursday and 22:00 to 09:00 on Fridays and Saturdays.

Pool

Swimming is not permitted after the following hours, as set out by the building's guidelines – Monday to Friday from 21:00 to 08:00 and on Saturdays and Sundays from 21:00 to 09:00

Recycling

There are refuse chutes in the stairwells between all floors which may be used between 08:00 and 21:00. Rubbish that is too big for the chutes must be put directly in the bins on B Block ground floor - take the elevator in the B Block down to level G.

Clean glass, plastic, cans, paper and cardboard are to be recycled and please leave these in the recycling bins near the rubbish chutes or in the marked bins on B Block ground floor.

Getting in touch

If something has gone wrong or if you need assistance, we will be more than happily to help out. You can contact us via email, byronandjasonhost@gmail.com, via WhatsApp on or via the Airbnb app.

Checking out

The check out time is 11:00. When checking out, remember to pop the keys and tag back into the lockbox, give the door of the lockbox a good push to close it securely and reset the combination to 4 random digits. To exit the building, ask the security at the Block A entrance to let you out of the main gate, as you won't have an access tag any more.
