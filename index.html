Location

Gardens is an affluent inner-city suburb of Cape Town located in the city centre on the higher elevations of the "City Bowl", a natural amphitheatre-shaped area bordered by Table Bay and surrounded by Signal Hill, Lion's Head, Table Mountain and Devil's Peak. This leafy neighbourhood is populated mostly by young professionals and boasts numerous chic restaurants, the luxurious Belmond Mount Nelson Hotel, various boutiques and plays host to both the Iziko Planetarium and the South African National Gallery.

Getting around

The easiest way to get around Cape Town is to use MyCiti, the local rapid transit bus service with over 37 routes and 366 stops, to Uber or to just stroll. Highly recommended are the tours on the open-top, double decker Hop on, Hop off bus, organised by City Sightseeing, Cape Town's official tourism arm. This tourism service is easy accessible from a stop just down the road from the apartment building — stop number 14.
